About Me

I grew up in Anchorage, Alaska. Son of an Air Force General's Daughter. I moved to Austin, Texas when I was 6 and visited my father every summer in Alaska until I was 13. All throughout my childhood I was an avid skateboarder. I was sponsered bu a local skate shop and even competed in competitions with big names like Tony Hawk, Christian Hosoi, and Steve Cabellerro when they rolled thru town on demo tours.

I went to a blue ribbon award winning high school in Austin. After high school I took a year off and eventually moved to Maui to stay with my uncle on his macadamia nut farm. During my time there I took up the sport of surfing and my life changed forever.

I went to college in Seattle, Washington at a community college in a town north of there named Everret. After I recieved my AAS I packed all of my belongings and moved to San Diego. I had every intention to finish my degree in Oceanography at Script Institute but the cost of living prohibited me from achieving that goal. Non-the-less I was happy living by the beach and surfing as often as work would permit.
